Deep garnet in the glass with a slight violet rim. The nose opens with cassis, dark cherry, and a hint of toasted cedar from barrel aging. Classic Napa Cab characteristics: concentrated fruit with well-integrated tannins.

On the palate, black currant and plum lead with layers of mocha and vanilla. The tannins are firm but polished, with a long, savory finish. A crowd-pleasing style built for pairing with grilled meats and aged cheeses.

Best enjoyed: now through 2028. Decant 30 min for full expression.
